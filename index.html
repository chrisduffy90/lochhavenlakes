Our Mission

Bridging the gap between community observation and civic action

The Loch Haven lake chain runs through the heart of Orlando, yet responsibility for its health is divided across multiple agencies. Fishing litter, water quality issues, and wildlife distress often go unreported — not because people don't care, but because they don't know who to call.

This platform exists to change that. Submit a report, track lake health over time, and connect with the right agency the first time.

Who manages what? The parks department oversees the shoreline, stormwater manages the first few feet of water, and Florida Fish & Wildlife covers the rest. A full jurisdiction guide is coming soon.
